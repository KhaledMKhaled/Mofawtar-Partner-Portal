==================================================
Unified Financial Items, Claims, and Settlements
==================================================

Simplify the financial workflow.

Do not use separate long status flows for:
- Order Payment Status
- Partner Commission Status
- Sales Commission Status

Instead, after every successfully Activated request, automatically create three financial items:

1. Payment Item
Type: payment_item
Purpose: collect the net amount due to the company from the partner after deducting partner commission.

2. Partner Commission Item
Type: partner_commission_item
Purpose: document and settle the partner commission that was deducted from the collected customer amount.

3. Sales Commission Item
Type: sales_commission_item
Purpose: document and settle the Sales user commission paid separately by the main company.

Each financial item must have one unified lifecycle status only:

1. not_added_to_claim
Arabic: غير مضافة على مطالبة

2. added_to_claim
Arabic: مضافة إلى مطالبة

3. added_to_settlement
Arabic: مضافة إلى تسوية

4. settled
Arabic: تمت التسوية

These four statuses apply to all financial item types:
- payment_item
- partner_commission_item
- sales_commission_item

Users must not manually change the financial item status directly.
The status must be updated automatically based on claim and settlement actions.

==================================================
Financial Item Creation on Activation
==================================================

When a request becomes Activated, the system must automatically create:

1. Payment Item
Amount = Net amount due to company
Formula:
Net amount due to company = Final price after tax - Partner commission amount

2. Partner Commission Item
Amount = Partner commission amount

3. Sales Commission Item
Amount = Sales commission amount, only if sales commission is enabled

All created financial items start with:

status = not_added_to_claim

Each financial item must store:
- Related SR
- Related customer
- Related partner
- Related sales user if applicable
- Related package
- Operation type
- Gross customer amount
- Item price before tax
- Tax amount
- Final price after tax
- Commission base
- Partner commission percentage
- Partner commission amount
- Sales commission percentage
- Sales commission amount
- Net amount due to company
- Financial item type
- Financial item status
- eligible_for_claim_at
- claim_id if added to claim
- settlement_id if added to settlement
- created_at
- settled_at

Example:
Item price before tax = 1000
Tax = 14%
Final price after tax = 1140
Partner commission = 20% of 1000 = 200
Sales commission = 5% of 1000 = 50
Net amount due to company = 1140 - 200 = 940

Create:
- payment_item amount = 940
- partner_commission_item amount = 200
- sales_commission_item amount = 50

==================================================
Claim Types
==================================================

The system must support three claim types:

1. Payment Claim
Type: payment_claim
Arabic: مطالبة مدفوعات
Purpose: group payment_item records to collect the net amount due from the partner to the company.

2. Partner Commission Claim
Type: partner_commission_claim
Arabic: مطالبة عمولة شريك
Purpose: group partner_commission_item records to document and approve the partner commission deduction.

3. Sales Commission Claim
Type: sales_commission_claim
Arabic: مطالبة عمولة مبيعات
Purpose: group sales_commission_item records to prepare sales commission payout from the company to Sales users.

Important:
A claim can include only financial items of the same type.

Examples:
- payment_claim can include payment_item records only.
- partner_commission_claim can include partner_commission_item records only.
- sales_commission_claim can include sales_commission_item records only.

Do not mix payment items, partner commission items, and sales commission items in the same claim.

When financial items are added to a claim:
- financial_item.status = added_to_claim
- claim_id is saved on each item
- audit log is created
- Customer 360 Timeline is updated

If a claim is rejected or cancelled before settlement:
- items return to not_added_to_claim
- reason is required
- audit log is created

==================================================
Settlement Types
==================================================

The system must support three settlement types:

1. Payment Settlement
Type: payment_settlement
Arabic: تسوية مدفوعات
Purpose: confirm that the company received the net amount due from the partner.

2. Partner Commission Settlement
Type: partner_commission_settlement
Arabic: تسوية عمولة شريك
Purpose: confirm that the partner commission deduction has been documented and settled.

3. Sales Commission Settlement
Type: sales_commission_settlement
Arabic: تسوية عمولة مبيعات
Purpose: confirm that the Sales commission payout has been completed.

A settlement can include one or more claims of the same type.

Examples:
- payment_settlement can include payment_claim records only.
- partner_commission_settlement can include partner_commission_claim records only.
- sales_commission_settlement can include sales_commission_claim records only.

When a claim is added to a settlement:
- all items inside the claim become added_to_settlement
- settlement_id is saved on each item
- audit log is created
- Customer 360 Timeline is updated

When the settlement is completed:
- all related financial items become settled
- settlement completed_at is saved
- claim status becomes settled
- audit log is created
- Customer 360 Timeline is updated

==================================================
Eligibility Rules
==================================================

Financial item status should remain simple.

Do not create extra status values for safety period or payout cycle.

Use eligibility fields instead:

- eligible_for_claim_at
- is_claimable
- claim_block_reason if not claimable

Payment Item:
- Usually claimable immediately after activation or once net amount due is calculated.

Partner Commission Item:
- Claimable only after the partner safety period ends.
- Safety period starts from request activation date.
- eligible_for_claim_at = activation date + safety period days.

Sales Commission Item:
- Claimable based on sales commission payout cycle.
- If payout cycle is monthly, eligible according to monthly payout rule.
- If payout cycle is every 3 months, eligible according to quarterly payout rule.

Before adding any financial item to a claim, the system must check:
- financial_item.status = not_added_to_claim
- current date >= eligible_for_claim_at
- financial item type matches claim type
- user has permission
- item is not cancelled or refunded
- related request is Activated

==================================================
Manual and Auto Claim Creation
==================================================

Both manual and auto modes must use the same structure.

Manual mode:
- Authorized user selects eligible financial items.
- User clicks Create Claim.
- System creates the claim and changes selected items to added_to_claim.

Auto mode:
- System periodically collects eligible financial items based on partner settings or payout cycle.
- System creates the claim automatically.
- System changes included items to added_to_claim.

In both modes:
- The financial item status is updated automatically.
- The user does not manually change the status.

==================================================
Balance Zeroing Logic
==================================================

The purpose of the financial workflow is to zero balances clearly.

For each activated request:

1. Company right from partner:
payment_item amount = Final price after tax - Partner commission amount

2. Partner commission documentation:
partner_commission_item amount = Partner commission amount

3. Sales commission payout:
sales_commission_item amount = Sales commission amount

Partner-company balance is closed when:
- payment_item is settled
- partner_commission_item is settled

Full financial closure is completed when:
- payment_item is settled
- partner_commission_item is settled
- sales_commission_item is settled, if sales commission exists

Sales commission can be settled later and independently.
It should not block partner-company balance closure.

Show clear balance indicators:
- Partner-Company Balance: Open / Closed
- Sales Payout: Pending / Closed
- Full Financial Status: Open / Fully Settled

==================================================
User Interface Rules
==================================================

Do not show many status dropdowns.

For financial items, show the unified status badge:
- غير مضافة على مطالبة
- مضافة إلى مطالبة
- مضافة إلى تسوية
- تمت التسوية

Show action buttons instead:

For claims:
- Create Payment Claim
- Create Partner Commission Claim
- Create Sales Commission Claim
- Approve Claim
- Reject Claim
- Move Claim to Settlement

For settlements:
- Create Payment Settlement
- Create Partner Commission Settlement
- Create Sales Commission Settlement
- Complete Settlement

Each action must:
- Validate permissions
- Validate item type
- Validate eligibility
- Update financial item status automatically
- Create audit log
- Update Customer 360 Timeline
- Trigger notifications

Reports and Dashboards
==================================================

Reports should use the simplified financial item status.

Filter financial items by:
- Financial item type
- Financial item status
- Partner
- Sales user
- Customer
- SR
- Claim
- Settlement
- Date range
- Eligibility date

Dashboards should show:

Payment Items:
- Not added to claim
- Added to claim
- Added to settlement
- Settled
- Total net due to company
- Total collected/settled

Partner Commission Items:
- Not added to claim
- Added to claim
- Added to settlement
- Settled
- Total partner commission
- Total documented/settled commission

Sales Commission Items:
- Not added to claim
- Added to claim
- Added to settlement
- Settled
- Total sales commission
- Total paid sales commission

Always separate:
- Payment Claims
- Partner Commission Claims
- Sales Commission Claims

Always separate:
- Payment Settlements
- Partner Commission Settlements
- Sales Commission Settlements
